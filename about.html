About BAZE

BAZE LTD Since January 1990 has facilitated Structural Engineering Solutions anchored on innovations relevant to the Nigerian Experience to deliver Projects in Civil / Structural Engineering in Industry and especially our Seminal work on Space Frames for Roofing Warehouses, Industrial Plants, General Purpose Halls plus even finding relevance for Towers and EOT Cranes / Conveyor Bridges.
Here employing Engineering Best Practices, we have evolved Patent Protected Systems Engineered to the Highest World Standards at very significant Cost Efficiencies.

All these in our fully equipped Structural Steel Fabrication Shops where we employ the full complement of Automated Steel Cutting, Punching and reaming systems, various Welding Procedures, EOT Cranes alongside other Fabrication Tools that are guided by Digital Imaging & transfer techniques in our Quality Control Systems which indeed approximates CNC systems. This Infrastructure backed by our world class QA / QC teams ensures that our completed assignments are exactly what they are intended to be.

Finally, our Corrosion Protection systems inclusive of Sand Blasting, Acid Etching / Prewash Tanks and a Zinc Galvanizing Kettle ensures Century long durability for our Products.

Meet The Team

We proudly introduce our very highly experienced and talented team.

ENGR. NNAMDI DIBIAEZUE

Managing Director

Engr. Nnamdi Dibiaezue (M.N.S.E) with over 25 years of practice experience ranging from Highways, Structural Engineering, Structural Steel Design and having held management level positions in the steel industry (Delta steel Company Ovwian/Aladja) brings to our clients a predilection and interest in innovation that has enabled him pioneer Steel Space Frame Design and Fabrication in Nigeria including Patents granted for space frame jointing techniques and Connection Systems for multi stand (up to 12 leg) Tower masts.

In addition; since 2006, he has pioneered use of hydraulic platforms with specially configured contact geometry in haulage of heavy duty power turbines and generators in excess of 320 tons over bridges designed to BS 5400 and therefore with theoretical load limits of 180 tons for various clients; including PANALPINA World Transport, ROCKSON Engineering, MGM Logistics Solutions, Exxon Mobil Producing Nigeria in Association with Suddeletra Nig Ltd e.t.c. This predilection enables him drive our innovations passionately as our Chief Executive Officer (C.E.O)

ENGR. FRANCIS EKHUEMELO

Director, Research and Corporate Liaison

Engr. Francis Ekhuemelo (MNSE, FNISET, FNATE, FNAEC): seasoned Engineer and Administrator with over 25 years of Management and Equivalent Board level experience in some of Nigeria’s most established and significant private and public corporations including, Delta Steel Company, Ajaokuta Steel Project, Shell Petroleum Development Cooperation and also at various times holding executive positions in prime engineering bodies like Nigerian Society of Engineers and culminating as VICE PRESIDENT Council for Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN) up to 2007. Engr. Ekhuemelo therefore brings this vast breath of knowledge to our Board of Directors as DIRECTOR, RESEARCH AND CORPORATE LIAISON.

MR. JOHNSON ODIETE

General Manager Crane, Installations and Maintenance

German trained expert in electric powered cranes both Electric Overhead Traveling (EOT), Gantry and Tower cranes, has served with DSD GmbH of Germany and for over 15 years was in charge of maintaining and revamping the vast fleet of EOT and Gantry cranes that serve DSC Aladja ranging from 3 ton Jib cranes to 200 ton Arc furnace Crane systems.

Since his entry into private practice he has revamped cranes in Delta Power Station, Ughelli; Egbin and Afam Power Stations as well as Dangote Sugar Refinery in Lagos.

MR. UGOCHUKWU ONWELUZO

Manager Project Planning and Construction Services

Mr. Ugochukwu Onweluzo (MICE) with over 15 years of experience in both our QA / QC functions and Project Management duties has the responsibility of Planning our Construction Services and oversees Site implementation.

MR. ADEBAYO LAZARUS
